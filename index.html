Missing Soundtrack?

Sometimes the situations you find yourself in simply beg for a theme song. No matter what you're experiencing, there is a song out there that will seem tailor-made for you. Finding that song is like fitting in a missing puzzle piece, tying together the movie that is your life, of which you are the star.

Check out three recommendations below...

For motivation, listen to the classic "It's my Life" by Bon Jovi. The New Jersey native captures the essence of living while you're alive.

It's now or never.

For a feel-good tune, look no farther than "Changes" by David Bowie. This throwaway-song-turned-major-hit is a bit more pop-oriented than his other music, but still has all the thought-provoking lyrics.

Time may change me, but I can't trace time.

For a song to get you moving, find "Killer Queen" by Queen. Freddie Mercury's vocals smoothly carry you through a fun song with a strong beat.

At anytime, an invitation you can't decline.
